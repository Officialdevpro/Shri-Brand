Arudhra Creations Private Limited

Women-led · Temple-inspired

Every day, South Indian temples receive beautiful flowers offered with reverence and devotion... Arudhra Creations Private Limited believes these flowers deserve a second, sacred life. We are a women-led enterprise based in Salem, Tamil Nadu, committed to transforming temple-blessed flowers into premium, carbon-free agarbathi sticks.

Women-Led Innovation

A skilled women’s team leads our flower processing, fragrance blending, and hand-rolling, creating dignified livelihoods across Salem.

Salem-Rooted, Temple-Focused

Based in Salem we partner with temples across the region to respectfully collect sacred flowers before disposal.

Purpose-Driven Model

We prevent sacred flowers from becoming waste, giving them a renewed spiritual purpose through eco-friendly, respectful transformation.
